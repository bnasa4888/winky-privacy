Privacy Policy — Winky

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Winky mobile application (the “App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use it. Winky turns your phone into a fullscreen signboard.

The data controller responsible for your information is:

Nasavan
Contact: support@nasavan.com

Winky is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

1. Information we collect

a. Account information (only if you sign in)

Signing in is optional — you may use the App as a guest. If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive and store the following from your Google account:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL
  • A Firebase user ID (a unique identifier for your account)

This data is stored in our database (Google Firebase / Firestore) and used to identify your account and link your subscription status to it.

b. Purchase and subscription information

If you purchase a subscription (Winky Pro), the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your purchase history and entitlement status (e.g. whether you have an active “pro” subscription), associated with your account identifier. We do not receive or store your full payment-card details — those are handled by Google Play.

c. Advertising data

Winky displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect and process your device’s advertising identifier and related device information. This data is shared with Google for advertising purposes. See Google’s handling of this data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

d. Information stored only on your device

Your signs, saved messages, theme choices, and app settings are stored locally on your device (via local app storage). This content stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

4. Data retention and deletion

We retain your account information for as long as your account exists. You can:

  • Sign out at any time from within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing support@nasavan.com. We will delete your account record and associated stored data within 30 days of a verified request.

Data stored only on your device can be removed by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the App.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict its processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@nasavan.com.

6. Security

We rely on industry-standard infrastructure (Google Firebase) to store account data. While no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
